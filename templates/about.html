About Watzup

Watzup is a social event discovery platform built for people who love experiences, connections, and real-life interactions. Whether you’re going out, networking, or hosting an event — Watzup helps you connect with the right people.

Built for Outgoing People

I’m an outgoing person who enjoys having my phone and discovering what’s happening around me. So I built Watzup — a place where people like us can find events, meet new people, and share experiences without limits or discrimination.

From parties to tech meetups, weddings to fitness events — every experience matters.

What Watzup Offers

🎟️ Event Discovery

Discover events around you or anywhere by switching location filters across states and local governments.

📢 Event Creation

Create free, private, or paid events so others can discover and join your experiences easily.

💰 Ticketing System

Sell tickets directly in-app. Watzup only takes a small 10% commission on ticket sales.

📍 Smart Location System

If no events exist in your area, Watzup automatically expands to your state — and beyond if needed.

👥 Attendee Management

Easily manage attendees inside the app — track, verify, and organize participants in real time.

🌍 No Discrimination

Every event type is welcome. Watzup is open, inclusive, and built for everyone.

Our Mission

To make discovering real-world experiences simple, social, and accessible. Watzup is built to connect people through events — not algorithms, not ads overload, but real human experiences.

The goal is simple: anywhere people are gathering, Watzup should make it discoverable.
